4.1 現在完成式 （Present Perfect Tense）

練習十一

將以下句子譯成英文，全部用現在完成式：

1.自從 1980 年，我就每天早上游泳。

1.I have been swimming every morning since 1980.

2.我已收到了你的信。

2.I have received your letter.

3.我從未去過美國。

3.I have never been to America.

4.從他是一個小孩開始，他就是一個基督徒（ Christian）。

4. He has been a Christian since he was a child.

5.我見過你的祖父。

5.I have seen your grandfather.

6.你的弟弟一直住在這裡。

6. Your brother has always lived here.

7.他學鋼琴已經很久了。

7. He has been learning to play the piano for a long time.

8.我已寫了三封信給他，他都沒有回。

8.I have already written three letters, but he didn't reply.

9.過去三年我都在開這部車。

9. For the past three years, I have driven this car.

10.自從 1975 年以來，他一直是一位老師。

10. He has been a teacher since 1975.

11.他教英文很久了。

11.He has taught English for a long time.

12.我曾經看過亂世佳人（ Gone with the Wind）。

12.I have seen Gone with the Wind.

13.我已吃過飯了。

13.I have eaten.

14.今年我去過海灘三次。

14.I have been to the beach three times this year.

15.我終其一生都愛你的。

15.I have loved you all my life.
