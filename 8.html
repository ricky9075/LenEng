3.2 過去進行式

練習八

將以下句子譯成英文，其中一部份使用過去進行式：

1.他昨天到學校去的時候，天在下雨。

1.It was raining when he went to school yesterday.

2.當火車停下的時候，他在看報（ read newspaper）。

2. He was reading a newspaper when the train stopped.

3.當火車進站的時候，他們在唱歌。

3. They were singing when the train arrived in the station.

4.我昨天去他家的時候，他在和他爸爸打電話。

4. He was calling his father when I went to his house.

5.昨天晚上八點鐘，我在家看電視。

5.I was watching TV when the clock struck eight lastnight.

6.當我爸爸昨晚打電話給我的時候，我正在刷牙（ brush my teeth）。

6.I was brushin my teeth when my father called me.

7.當我昨晚打電話給他時，他在游泳。

7. He was swimming when I called him.

8.當這貓走進來的時候，那隻狗在睡覺。

8. That dog was sleeping when the cat came in.

9.當他弟弟回家的時候，他在拉小提琴。

9. He was playing the violin when his younger brother came home.

10.當我爸爸回家的時候，我媽媽在燒飯。

10. My mother was cooking dinner when my father came home.

11.他去年非常虛弱。

11. He was very weak last year.

12.他正在打電話。

12. He is making a phone call now.

13.他每天都努力工作（ work hard）。

13. He works hard every day.

14.你的弟弟喜歡游泳。

14. Your younger brother likes swimming.

15.他過去是個好學生。

15. He was a good student before.
