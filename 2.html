2.1 現 在 式

練習二

將以下中文句子翻成英文，都用現在式。

1.他是一個好學生。

1. He.is a good student.

2.我的哥哥 17 歲。

2. My elder brother is seventeen years old.

3.我弟弟每天游泳。

3. My younger brother swims every day.

4.他們都喜歡音樂。

4. All of them like music.

5.他們現在在日本。

5. They are in Japan now.

6.喜瑪拉雅山是全世界最高的山。

6. Jade Mountain is a very high mountain.

7.亞馬遜河是全世界最長的。

7. The Amazon River is a long river.

8.他的爸爸是位老師。

8. His father is a teacher.

9.我們都喜歡中國菜。

9. All of us like Chinese food.

10.他不喜歡冰淇淋。

10. He doesn't like ice cream.

11.我愛你。

11.I love you.

12.每個人都怕蛇。

12. Everyone is afraid of snakes.

13.每個人都喜歡狗。

13. Everyone likes dogs.

14.今天真冷。

14. It is very cold today.
