練習七

將以下句子譯成英文，用現在式，現在進行式或過去式：

1.他在打籃球。

1. He is playing basketball now.

2.他喜歡打籃球。

2. He likes playing basketball.

3.他昨天打籃球。

3. He played basketball yesterday.

4.他每天騎腳踏車上學。

4. He goes to school by bicycle every day.

5.他昨天騎腳踏車到鄉下去。

5. He went to the country by bicycle yesterday.

6.我喜歡唱歌。

6.I like singing.

7.他正在唱歌。

7. I watched two movies yesterday.

8.你的爸爸昨天來看我。

8. Your father came to see me yesterday.

9.他的哥哥每天步行二公里。

9. His older brother walks two kilometers.every day.

10.他的弟弟是個好孩子。

10. His younger brother is a good boy.

11.他去年非常虛弱。

11. He was very weak last year.

12.他正在打電話。

12. He is making a phone call now.

13.他每天都努力工作（ work hard）。

13. He works hard every day.

14.你的弟弟喜歡游泳。

14. Your younger brother likes swimming.

15.他過去是個好學生。

15. He was a good student before.
